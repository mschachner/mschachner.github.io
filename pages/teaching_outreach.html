Teaching

I love to teach! I have four semesters of experience teaching at Cornell:

  • in autumn 2022, I was a recitation teaching assistant for MATH 1910 Calculus II for Engineers;
  • in spring 2023, I was a recitation teaching assistant for MATH 2210 Linear Algebra;
  • in autumn 2023, I was head TA / recitation TA for MATH 2940 Linear Algebra for Engineers; and
  • in spring 2024, I was instructor of record for MATH 1120 Calculus II.

I'm not teaching now, but plan to participate in the Cornell Prison Education Program in spring 2025.

Outreach

In the summers of 2019 and 2020, I was a teaching assistant/counselor for BEAM, an outreach program which helps increase the accessibility of advanced mathematics for underserved students.

I'm active in Cornell's chapter of the Association for Women in Mathematics, and served on the professional development team in spring 2024.

In autumn 2022, spring 2024, and autumn 2024, I have been a mentor to multiple undergraduate students as part of the Cornell mathematics directed reading program.

In the spring of 2023, I was an instructor for Little Circle, a Cornell-run outreach program for students in grades 3-5. We learned all about infinity, fractals, and more.
